The House of Hohenzollern is a dynasty of former princes, electors, kings, and emperors of Hohenzollern, Brandenburg, Prussia, the German Empire, and Romania. The House of Hohenzollern came to the throne of Brandenburg in 1415. Subsequent members of the Hohenzollern family ruled until 1918 in Berlin, first as electors of Brandenburg. At the end of World War I, monarchy and aristocracy was overthrown and Germany became a republic, known as the Weimar Republic.
